About Me

Hi, my name is Emmanuel Klu. I am an experienced designer, developer, researcher, entrepreneur and overall thinker based in Chicago, IL. I am currently studying computer science with a minor in psychology at the Illinois Institute of Technology, and will be graduating in May 2013. I have been focusing my efforts on software engineering and research in cognitive science.

In the computer science space, I have strong interests in Human Computer Interaction, Artificial Intelligence, Cognitive Science and User Experience. Background

I grew up in Ghana, West Africa, where I got into design, web development, and entrepreneurship at a very young age. In high school I established my first company, which offered services in web development, graphic design, branding, and product development. Also, I got exposed to programming (Ruby), but I really got a good feel of it when I came to college.

My design and web development skills are all self taught - Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, .NET, PHP, Javascript, and SQL. Most of my programming skills are self taught also. I started focusing more on software development after taking my first programming class (Java) in college. Since then I have taken courses in data structures, algorithms, programming languages, systems, databases and computer organization.
