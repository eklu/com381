About Me

Hi, my name is Emmanuel Klu. I am an experienced designer and developer based in the Bay Area, California. I did high school at Mfantsipim, and I graduated with a bachelor's in computer science and a minor in psychology from the Illinois Tech in 2013. I work as a software engineer at Google.

Background

I grew up in Cape Coast, Ghana, where I got into design, web development, and entrepreneurship at a very young age. In high school I established my first company, which offered services in web development, graphic design, branding, and product development. I took some initial steps towards software development, but I only really got a good feel of it in college.

My design and web development skills are all self taught. Most of my programming skills are self taught also. I started focusing more on software development after taking my first programming class in college.
