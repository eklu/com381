Interests

Travel

People hate being tourists, but I absolutely love the idea of backpacking. Since I started college, I have been to 21 of the 50 states, and DC. I have backpacked around western Europe - England, France, Italy, Switzerland, Germany, and the Netherlands - covering all the major cities. I am looking forward to exploring more of eastern Europe come summer 2013. I love nature and the amazing artchitecture here and around the world. An interesting fact is, I have not travelled to any other African country besides my homeland.

Music

I love jazz, blues, christian and country music - pretty old school I know. I play the piano and know a little about the bass guitar. People ask what kind of music I play, and I tell them "anything", but really, I play and contemporary christian music mostly. Unfortunately, I cannot read the staff and play simultaneously. I do read chord music sheets to play, but I mostly improvise and play by ear. I enjoy playing the piano in my free time and when I am stressed out. Hopefully I can play for charity someday.

Sports AND FITNESS
